McDonald's E.Coli Crisis Response Strategy

Consulting Case Study

Project Overview

Comprehensive consulting analysis addressing McDonald's October 2024 E.coli outbreak crisis. Our team developed a multi-faceted recovery strategy encompassing food safety improvements, stakeholder communication, and brand reputation management.

Key Deliverables

  • Strategic crisis containment framework
  • 5-point implementation plan for food safety enhancement
  • Stakeholder communication strategy across 6 channels
  • Risk mitigation roadmap with ROI projections
  • Competitive analysis of industry precedents (Wendy's, Chipotle)

Impact & Insights

Research revealed 66% of consumers would return immediately after proper crisis response, presenting significant retention opportunity. Our recommendations leveraged this data to design recovery initiatives aligned with consumer expectations for transparency and safety.

NCAA Athletics Financial Analysis Platform

Database Management

Project Overview

Designed and implemented a normalized relational database analyzing 300+ NCAA athletic programs across multiple divisions. Built interactive web application using Oracle APEX for dynamic data exploration and visualization.

Technical Implementation

  • 5-table normalized schema with proper foreign key relationships
  • Cleaned and sampled dataset from 15,000+ to 300 records
  • Complex queries including subqueries, joins, and aggregations
  • Interactive dashboards with filtering and drill-down capabilities

Key Findings

  • Division I FBS programs spend 4.5M+ annually vs <250K for other divisions
  • Only football and men's basketball generate positive revenue margins
  • Gender participation parity exists, but revenue disparities remain significant

Ames Housing Market Analytics

Business Analytics

Project Overview

Predictive modeling analysis of 2,930 residential property sales in Ames, Iowa (2006-2010). Developed multiple regression model to identify key price drivers and inform real estate investment decisions.

Analytical Approach

  • Evaluated 80+ variables for correlation with sale price
  • Selected 9 optimal predictors balancing power and parsimony
  • Multiple linear regression with R² = 0.893
  • Residual analysis confirming model assumptions

Key Insights

  • Living area is the strongest single predictor (r=0.786)
  • Each additional year of age decreases value by $45.74
  • Basement space shows strong ROI: +$40.41 per sq ft
  • Model explains 89.3% of price variance
